research group introduction

introduction:

The Plant Genomics and Computational Biology Research Group, a team of researchers set up by Dr. Zhu Andan in 2017. At present,it is mainly through the method of combining computational biologyand experiment, to study the diversity of wild species resources and the formation mechanism of special and important horticultural traits from the perspectives of genomic complexity, multi-polygenome interaction, gene expression regulation and gene function analysis.

key research content:


1) Models and mechanisms of nucleo-plasmic co-evolution, Including the law of structural variation of plant mitochondrial genome and its relationship with variation rate and recombination, the role of nucleo-cytoplasmic interaction in seed development, etc.
2)Plant polyploid formation and expression of regulatory mechanisms, including the dose effect of ploidy variation, Cis or trans regulatory variation, And the role of epigenetic modification in important flower resources and the development seeds.
3)the collection and utilization of characteristic wild seed resources.
