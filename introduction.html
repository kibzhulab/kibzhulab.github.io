research group introduction

introduction:

The Plant Genomics and Computational Biology Research Group set up by Dr. Zhu Andan in 2017. At present,we mainly through the method of computational biology binding experiment, to study the diversity of germplasm resources in wild plants and the formation mechanism of special and important horticultural traits from the perspectives of genomic complexity, multi-polygenome interaction, gene expression regulation and gene function analysis.

key research content:


1) Models and mechanisms of nucleo-plasmic co-evolution, including the law of structural variation of plant mitochondrial genome and its relationship with variation rate and recombination, the role of nucleo-cytoplasmic interaction in seed development, etc.
2)Plant polyploid formation and expression of regulatory mechanisms, including the dose effect of ploidy variation, cis or trans regulatory variation, the role of epigenetic modification in important flower resources and the development seeds.
3)The collection and utilization of characteristic wild seed resources.
