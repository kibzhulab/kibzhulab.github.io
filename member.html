Research Interests:

Plant Comparative and Evolutionary Genomics. Combining state-of-the-art sequencing techniques and molecular biology to study the evolution of genetic and trait innovations, such as photosynthsis and energy-related metabolism, genomic structure, gene and intron content, RNA editing, horizontal gene transfer, etc.
