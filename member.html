Research direction:

Plant comparison genomics with evolutionary biology. Based on the combination of genomic and transcriptome sequencing techniques and molecular biology experiments, the evolutionary characteristics of the genome of the green phytoplasm and the origin and evolution of some new features are systematically analyzed: such as genomic structure, gene and inclusion content, RNA editing, horizontal gene transfer, etc., At the same time, the polyploid genome was studied and the effects of cis-inverse regulation on genes and their expression were studied.

"Hundred Talents Program" of The Chinese Academy of Sciences
