Dr. Weishu Fan, Assistant Professor

Email: fanweishu@mail.kib.ac.cn

Learning and work experience:

2018.1- Assistant Professor, Germplasm Bank of Wild Species, Kunming Institute of Botany(KIB), Chinese Academy of Sciences(CAS).
2012.8-2017.9 Ph.D. and Postdoctoral, Department of Agronomy and Horticulture, University of Nebraska-Lincoln, USA.
2007.9-2011.7 Bachelor of Agriculture, Agricultural College, Northwest Agricultural and Forestry University.

Research Interests:

Plant comparative and evolutionary genomics. Combining next-generation sequencing techniques and molecular biology experiments to explore the evolutionary characteristics of land plants, and study the origin and evolution of new features, such as genomic structure, gene and intron content, RNA editing, horizontal gene transfer, etc., Meanwhile, the polyploid genome and the effects of sequence divergence on gene expression are studied.
