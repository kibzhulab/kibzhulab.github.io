Dr. Andan Zhu is a professor at the Kunming Institute of Botany, Chinese Academy of Sciences, and PI of the Plant Genomics and Computational Biology Research Group. Before that, he got his Ph.D. in Huazhong Agricultural University in 2012, followed by postdoctoral trainings at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln and University of Texas-Austin. He has now published over 30 peer-reviewed articles, such as Nature Genetics, Molecular Biology and Evolution, Plant Cell, New phytologist, etc.
